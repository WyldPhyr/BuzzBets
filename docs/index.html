The Problem

My end-users are people who want to explore a controversial topic in-depth. Right now most people are very one-sided in their beliefs because that is what they’re accustomed to; it’s the culture they grew up in. Another demographic my app will assist are the argumentative folks out there that always need to be right. Currently, they are reduced to bluffing about data and statistics, but with this app, all the data they need will be at their fingertips, conveniently sorted by topic and which side it supports. The app I will create will need to be accessible to the general public (intuitive), yet detailed enough for enthusiastic debaters.

The Rationale

I decided to create an app that provides statistics and other details on controversial topics. I chose to work in Python, because of my personal familiarity with it, and the for the Kivy resource to help develop the app for multiple platforms. Also, I may work on this project with the “client,” who is new to programming, so I need a programming language that is easy on beginners.
