IB Internal Assessment for Computer Science

Success Criteria for the Internal Assessment

  • It must be intuitive (test on someone unfamiliar with the application/project)
  • It must run off of real data (based on Google Trends trending searches)
  • It must allow users to bet whether the search term will be more or less popular in the future
  • It must show the user performance of their portfolio over time
  • It must allow users to see "more details" on specific search terms:
    • Graphs of past performance
    • Percentage gained/lost over time
    • Related news articles
  • It must have "Home," "Explore," "Watchlist," and "Portfolio" tabs.
    • With the ability to search for specific terms in the "Explore" tab
    • With the biggest gains and losses of the day in the "Explore" tab
